TWiT – "This Week In Tech" – The most popular podcast?

By Phil Whelan on October 17, 2005

  • Tweet
  • Tweet

I’m enjoying listening to these fun hour long discussions from Leo Laporte’s gang on anything tech related. Currently they’re doing it just for the love of it, but with a quarter of a million listeners and a growing fan-base will they sell out and go commercial? They don’t think so.

You can subscribe to the TWiT podcast through iTunes, or download the podcast MP3′s directly from the TWiT website.

You can also download these as large (approx 500Mb) movie files using bittorrent from Revision3.

Azureus is currently my favorite bittorrent client.
